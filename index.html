Project Inspiration

My story

The inspiration for my piano project stems from a long-standing, unfulfilled dream of learning to play this beautiful instrument. Throughout my life, I've been captivated by the grace and elegance of piano music, often finding myself in awe of those who could effortlessly bring melodies to life. Watching pianists play felt like witnessing a master artist at work, each note painting an exquisite auditory masterpiece. Despite my deep-seated envy and admiration, the opportunity to learn the piano always seemed just out of reach.

Dreams to Reality!

Inspired by my deep desire to learn piano and the requirements of the Master of Education contest, I decided to create a piano tutor from scratch. Thus, Beky. Utilizing 3D-printed unweighted piano keys, interpreting notes from MIDI files, and simulating sound on a $35 Raspberry Pi, Beky aims to be an accessible and effective learning tool. It’s designed to help anyone progress from a complete beginner to a proficient mozart-like player with consistent practice.
